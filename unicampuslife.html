CAMPUS LIFE

INFRASTRUCTURE

FlinTech is designed by Kaycee Mate, the American architecture firm which specialises in educational institutions state of art libraries,labs,auditoriums,outdoor fields and gyms are some of the beauties of this university We guarantee that our campus will be a home to you, nurture you, inspire you, and forever be an indelible part of you

HOSTEL

we provide Two separate exclusive boys and girls hostel with 2 & 3 Bedded accommodation having Optional A/C facility .The rooms are in a 2/3/4 sharing format. we also include WIFI connectivity,24/7 in house medical care unit and ambulance facility available. There is Open air movie screening facility for weekly movie shows other facilities include:Exclusive visitors lounge, 24/7 security vigilance,Treated RO & Cool water in all the floors,Solar hot water for bathing,Good house-keeping and green ambience.

CANTEEN

Well designed spacious and spotlessly maintained dining halls catering to different needs of students. We have special, north Indian, south Indian, vegetarian, non-vegetarian categories of food served in different messes. Special menus are provided during festival occasions. Separate canteen is also available to cater to the needs of the students.

GENNOVATION LAB

Advancements in technology are happening every single day. Students around the world are being exposed to the advantages of learning robotics . All the sudents will be given practical training. Student will enjoy exploring the robotics with us. Most of our students are coming up with their own innovative robotics projects. .

TOURS AND VISITS

At FlinTech we provide education through practical fields of work too. Regular tours include t-hub visit every 3 months to understand work environment better,learning and picking up work ettiqute, Keeping up with latest technologies. For the entertainment of students we take them on an annual excursion .We also encourage the students to take part in events off campus concerning social welfare

FESTS

SPACE – a national-level two-day cultural and sports festival is by far the largest cultural festival of the university and since its inception has served as a pioneer of art and culture for the youth. It is a world where people will be overwhelmed to enter but never think of exit. Through the years providing a “one of it’s kind” experience has been the predicament of the festival and achieving it every year has been our legacy.

SPORTS

To achieve a healthier lifestyle one needs to be healthy. As a part of this process, FlinTech offers: cricket,football,basketball,badminton,tennis and volleyball as outdoor activities. Swimming,carroms,chess and Table tennis as indoor activities. Its numerous outdoor playgrounds and indoor courts are spread over a vast area. Regular inter-college games and events are held to maintain healthy competition.We also have a Gym.
