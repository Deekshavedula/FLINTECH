Electronics and Communication Engineering

The department of Electronics & Communication Engineering started right from the inception of the college in the year 1997. The primary objective of the department has been to impart quality education, training and research at the undergraduate and postgraduate in various areas of Electronics and Communication Engineering The Department presently offers UG Programme B.Tech The Department has been accredited by NBA in 2006, 2009, 2014 and 2017 for excellence in engineering education.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering

The department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering started right from the inception of the college in the year 1997. The primary objective of the department has been to impart quality education, training and research. The Department presently offers UG Programme B.Tech . The Department has been accredited by NBA in 2006, 2009, 2014 and 2017 for excellence in engineering education.

Computer Science and Engineering

Computer Science and Engineering Deparment started in the year 1997. It conducts B.Tech programmes . The Department has well- equipped laboratories. It focuses on practical applications in the field of Data Mining and Image Processing. The Department is accredited by NBA in 2006, 2009 and 2014. In addition to quality teaching, students are exposed to certification courses and workshops along with personality development programs.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering department commenced offering UG programme BTech (Mechanical Engineering) in 1997. The department has been recognized by JNTUH as Research Center. The Department has requisite number of faculty members, striving to incorporate excellence in theory, practice and research. Throughout the curriculum, design courses, laboratory courses, and project courses allow students to acquire a taste for the complex task of designing, analyzing, and building a device.

Civil Engineering

The department of Civil Engineering started in the year 2008. The primary objective of the department has been to impart quality education, training and research at the undergraduate and postgraduate in Structural Engineering with broad emphasis on design aspects of roads and building . The Department presently offers UG Programme B.Tech The Department has been accredited by NBA in 2016 for three years and reaccredited in 2019 UG and 2018 for PG , for excellence in engineering education.
